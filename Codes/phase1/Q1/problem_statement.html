Vedant wants to buy some Somaiya sheets but he is short of pocket money! The Somaiya sheets costs C Rupees.

Vedant has a magical piggy bank. He soon discovered that he could put some money into it each morning and in the dark of the night the money contained in the piggy bank would become X times. He could deposit any amount of money on any day. He might not deposit any money on some days. Regardless of all this the money in the piggy bank would become X times every night.

Now Vedant wants to deposit the least possible total sum of money into the piggy bank and obtain C Rupees. Being the good boy Vedant is, he doesn't aspire for anything more and hence needs exactly C Rupees when he breaks the piggy bank on some afternoon. Note that the number of days he puts money or when he breaks the piggy bank is not required to be minimised.

Input

First line contains two integers C and X.

Output

Output in a single line one integer the minimum total amount of money Vedant needs to add to Piggy bank to get exactly C Rupees on some day.

Constraints

  • 1 <= C <= 109
  • 1 <= X <= 109

Sample Input

5 3

Sample Output

3
